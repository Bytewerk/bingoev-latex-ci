Corporate Identity package for Bingo e.V.
=========================================

What does it do?
----------------
This package tries to make your documents match the corporate identity of
Bingo e.V..
The font is changed to what is considered a sane choice for documents, easily
readable on both laptop screens and paper. A logo is inserted in the upper
right corner, and the full name of the association is inserted in the upper
left corner.

Options for this package:
-------------------------

 * impressum
	Print the impressum in the page footer.
 * letterhead
 	Show the name and logo of Bingo e.V. in the page header.
